Git Definitions

In these definitions, the usual part of speech of the word is shown. As is often the case with developer terminology, most of these terms can be nouns or verbs depending on the circumstance. I've shown the most common use that I've seen. If a word is commonly used as both a noun and a verb, I've denoted the term with both.
